About FoodEx

Mission Statement

We live to connect. Our goal is to solve the national hunger problem by providing easy access to free relief resources.

How We Help

FoodEx is a service that connects food banks with people in need of assistance. By streamlining the signup process, food banks can focus on providing relief to those who qualify. Users can search for food banks in their area, and schedule a pickup at a convenient time.

Why it matters

When someone uses a food bank, they usually have to go through a finance and background check for eligibility. FoodEx hopes to make this process as easy and anonymous as possible, helping you fix your circumstances with helpful resources. We also want to make the process of getting food as fast as possible since we know you have a busy schedule, so we have an optional scheduler to pick up food.
